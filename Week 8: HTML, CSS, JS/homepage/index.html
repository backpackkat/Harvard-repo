Start Here: an Overview

  • Dance is a performing art form consisting of sequences of movement, either improvised or choreographed. This movement has aesthetic and often symbolic value.
    Dance can be categorized and described by its choreography, by its repertoire of movements, or by its historical period or place of origin.
    Other forms of human movement are sometimes said to have a dance-like quality, including martial arts, gymnastics, cheerleading, figure skating, synchronised swimming, marching bands, and many other forms of athletics. There are 10 main traditional types of dance styles, not counting all the fusions that combine multiple styles. More are coming out all the time.
